  ## toki pi nanpa wan - The Shma
  <span class="translation">jan Isale o kute e ni! jan sewi wan pi mi mute li lawa!</span>  
  <span class="literal">Israel, hear this! Our one G-d leads!</span>

  ## ma tomo Pape - Babel Text
  > If you would like to see the original English text as it appears in the Bible, go [here](http://omniglot.com/babel/english.htm).

  <span class="translation">jan ali li kepeken e toki <Change till={1}>wan</Change> sama.</span>  
  <span class="literal">Everyone used the same, common language.</span>

  <span class="translation">jan li kama tan nasin pi kama suno li kama tawa ma Sinale li awen lon ni.</span>  
  <span class="literal">People came from the road of the sun's approach and came to Shinar and stayed there.</span>

  <span class="translation">jan li toki e ni: "o kama! mi mute o pali e kiwen. o seli e ona."</span>  
  <span class="literal">People said this: "Come! Let's make bricks. Let's warm them."</span>

  <span class="translation">jan mute li toki e ni: "o kama! mi mute o pali e tomo mute e tomo palisa suli. sewi pi tomo palisa li lon sewi kon. nimi pi mi mute o kama suli! mi wile ala e ni: mi mute li lon ma ante mute."</span>  
  <span class="literal">Many people said this: "Come! Let's make many building and a big tower. The top of the tower will be in heaven. May our name become important! I don't want this: We are in many different lands."</span>

  <span class="translation">jan sewi Jawe li kama anpa li lukin e ma tomo e tomo palisa.</span>  
  <span class="literal">The Lord came down and saw the city and the tower.</span>

  <span class="translation">jan sewi Jawe li toki e ni: <Change from={1}>"</Change>jan li lon ma wan li kepeken e toki sama li pali e tomo palisa. tenpo ni la ona <Change till={1}>mute</Change> li ken pali e ijo ike mute.</span>  
  <span class="literal">And the Lord said this: The people are in one area and use the same language and have made a tower. Now they can do many bad things.</span>

  <span class="translation">mi wile tawa anpa li wile pakala e toki pi jan mute ni. mi wile e ni: jan li sona ala e toki pi jan ante.</span>  
  <span class="literal">I want to go down and want to destroy the language of these many people. I want this: People don't understand the language of other people.</span>

  <span class="translation">jan sewi Jawe li kama e ni: jan li lon ma mute li ken ala pali e tomo.</span>  
  <span class="literal">The Lord caused this: People are in many lands and can't build buildings.</span>

  <span class="translation">nimi pi ma tomo ni li Pape tan ni: jan sewi Jawe li pakala e toki pi jan ali. <Change till={1}>tan ma tomo Pape la</Change> jan sewi Jawe li tawa e jan tawa ma mute<Change from={1}> tan ma tomo Pape</Change></span>  
  <span class="literal">The name of this city is Babel because of this: the Lord destroyed the language of everyone. <Change till={1}>From the city Babel, the</Change><Change from={1}>The</Change> Lord moved people to many lands<Change from={1}> from the city Babel</Change></span>

  ## toki lawa mute - The Ten Commandments
  <span class="translation">mi taso li jan sewi sina.</span>  
  <span class="literal">Only I am your god.</span>
  
  <span class="translation">o pali ala e ijo sewi lili. o sewi ala e ona.</span>  
  <span class="literal">Don't make little religious objects. Don't worship them.</span>
  
  <span class="translation">o kepeken sewi e nimi mi.</span>  
  <span class="literal">Use religiously my name.</span>
  
  <span class="translation">tenpo suno sewi li suli.</span>  
  <span class="literal">The holy day is important.</span>
  
  <span class="translation">mama sina li lawa e sina.</span>

  <span class="translation">o wile ala e ijo pi jan ante.</span>  
  <span class="literal">Don't want other people's things.</span>
  
  ## mama pi mi mute - The Lord's Prayer                
  <span class="translation">mama pi mi mute o, sina lon sewi kon.</span>  
  <span class="literal">Our father, You are in heaven.</span>

  <span class="translation">nimi sina li sewi.</span>  
  <span class="literal">Your name is holy.</span>

  <span class="translation">ma sina o kama.</span>  
  <span class="literal">May your kingdom come.</span>

  <span class="translation">jan o pali e wile sina lon sewi kon en lon ma.</span>  
  <span class="literal">May people do your will in heaven and in the world.</span>

  <span class="translation">o pana e moku pi tenpo suno ni tawa mi mute.</span>  
  <span class="literal">Give the food of today to us.</span>

  <span class="translation">o weka e pali ike mi. sama la <Change till={2}>o</Change><Change from={2}>mi</Change> weka e pali ike pi jan ante.</span>  
  <span class="literal">Remove my bad deeds. Similarly, remove the bad deeds of other people.</span>

  <span class="translation">o <Change till={2}>kama e ni: mi wile ala ike.</Change><Change from={2}>lawa ala e mi tawa ike.</Change></span>  
  <span class="literal">Cause this: I don't want evilly.</span>

  <span class="translation">o lawa e mi tan ike.</span>  
  <span class="literal">Lead me from evil.</span>

  <span class="translation">tenpo ali la ma en ken en pona li pi sina. </span>  
  <span class="literal">Always the land and the ability and the greatness is of you. Amen.</span>

  ## toki sona pi nasin sewi Silami - Islamic Declaration of Faith
  <span class="translation">jan sewi wan taso li jan sewi. jan Muwama li jan pali ona li jan toki ona.</span>  
  <span class="literal">Only one god is God (Allah). Muhammad is his servant and his messenger.</span> 
  
  ## toki pi jan Puta - A Quote by Buddha
  <span class="translation">ilo suno wan li ken pana e suno tawa ilo suno mute mute. wawa pi ilo suno li kama ala lili. jan li pona tawa jan mute la pona li kama ala lili.</span>   
  <span class="literal">One candle can give light to thousands of candles. The strength of the candle doesn't become decreased. When someone is good to many people, the goodness doesn't become smaller. </span>
  

  ## toki sewi pi jan walo - Five Fundamental Beliefs of Creativity
  <span class="translation">mi mute li sona e ni: kulupu jan li nasin sewi.</span>  
  <span class="literal">We know that race is religion.</span>

  <span class="translation">mi mute li sona e ni: jan walo li pona mute. jan ante li ike.</span>  
  <span class="literal">We know that white people are very good. Other people are evil.</span>

  <span class="translation">mi mute li sona e ni: jan li pona tawa jan walo la jan ni li pona <Change from={1}>mute</Change>. jan li pakala e jan walo la jan ni li ike mute.</span>  
  <span class="literal">We know that if a person is good to the white people, that person is good. If a person harms the white people, that person is very evil.</span>

  <span class="translation">mi mute li sona e ni: nasin li pona tawa jan walo la ona li pona mute. nasin li ike tawa jan walo la ona li ike mute.</span>  
  <span class="literal">We know that if a path is good for the white people, it is the ultimate good. If a path is bad for the white people, it is the ultimate evil.</span>

  <span class="translation">mi mute li sona e ni: nasin pali li nasin sewi taso pi jan walo. ona taso li ken sewi e jan walo.</span>  
  <span class="literal">We know that Creativity is the only religion for the white people. Only it can raise the white people.</span>

  <span class="translation">mi mute li wile sewi. mi mute li pali tan ni.</span>  
  <span class="literal">We must be victorious. We work because of this.</span>

  <span class="translation">utala sewi pi kulupu jan a!</span>  
  <span class="literal">O holy war of the races!</span>
